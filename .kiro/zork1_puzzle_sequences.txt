## 5. CYCLOPS PUZZLE (Scare cyclops away)
================================================================================
Starting location: West of House
Goal: Reach Cyclops Room and scare cyclops away using Odyssey reference
Success indicator: "The cyclops, who is apparently of Homeric ancestry,
remembers the dread Ulysses and flees through the wall..."

IMPORTANT NOTE: The cyclops is NOT fed. You scare him away by saying
"ULYSSES" or "ODYSSEUS" (referencing The Odyssey where Odysseus/Ulysses
blinded Polyphemus the cyclops). This creates a passage east to the
Living Room.

After "se" (entering Cyclops Room):
"Cyclops Room
This room has an exit on the northwest, and a staircase leading up.

A cyclops, who looks remarkably like a refugee from a late-night
horror movie, is standing in the corner. He doesn't look happy."

After "ulysses":
"The cyclops, who is apparently of Homeric ancestry, remembers the
dread Ulysses and flees through the wall, which opens as he goes,
revealing a new exit to the east."

AFTER CYCLOPS FLEES:
- New exit EAST leads directly to Living Room (shortcut!)
- Staircase UP leads to Treasure Room (Thief's lair)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ALTERNATE COMMANDS THAT WORK:
- odysseus
- say ulysses
- say odysseus
